✦ Timeline of the Ancient World ✦

From the birth of civilization to the fall of empires.

3500 BCE

Sumer rises in Mesopotamia. The first cities, the first writing, the first gods with names.

3100 BCE

Egypt unified under Pharaoh Narmer. The age of pyramids and divine kings begins.

2700 BCE

The Epic of Gilgamesh is composed — the oldest written story in human history.

2560 BCE

The Great Pyramid of Giza is completed. A monument to eternity that still stands.

1200 BCE

The Trojan War. Homer's Iliad and Odyssey preserve the myths of gods and heroes.

800 BCE

The Norse sagas begin to take shape in Scandinavia. Odin, Thor, and Loki enter legend.

500 BCE

The golden age of Greek philosophy. Socrates, Plato, and Aristotle walk the earth.

200 BCE

The Mahabharata and Ramayana are written in India — the greatest epics of Hindu mythology.

100 CE

The Aztec and Maya civilizations flourish in Mesoamerica, building temples to the sun and stars.

700 CE

The Norse Eddas are compiled. The myths of Ragnarok, creation, and the nine worlds are preserved.
